Description

Watermelon, Cucumber, and Mint Salad is a refreshing dish made with juicy watermelon, cool cucumbers, and fresh mint leaves. It's a very simple and easy recipe that combines the sweetness of watermelon, the crispness of cucumbers, and the delightful aroma of mint. This salad is perfect for hot summer days when you want something light and hydrating. It's a delicious and healthy option for everyone to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 large seedless watermelon, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 12 leaves fresh mint, thinly sliced
  • Â¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • coarse salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Gently mix watermelon, cucumbers, and mint in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat.
  4. Add feta cheese and gently mix.
