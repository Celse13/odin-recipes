Jamaican Beef Patties


Description

Jamaican Beef Patties are delicious handheld pastries filled with savory ground beef and flavorful spices. They are like little pies that you can hold in your hand and eat. The pastry dough is soft and flaky, while the beef filling is seasoned with tasty spices that give it a special kick. These patties are perfect for snacking on during outdoor games or as a satisfying treat after a party.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (savory)
  • Spices (for a flavorful kick)
  • Dough (used for the pastry)

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling
  2. Make the dough
  3. Assemble the patties
  4. Bake or fry the patties
  5. Serve and enjoy
